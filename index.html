About

Engineering knowledge meets clear writing

I'm a Technical Writer and Documentation Specialist with a B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering and hands-on experience across GMP-regulated pharma, ISO-certified manufacturing, industrial automation, and electrical systems.

My engineering background isn't a footnote — it's what lets me sit with subject matter experts, ask the right questions, and translate complex machine logic into documentation that technicians actually use in the field.

I manage full draft → review → approval → submittal cycles, coordinate cross-functional review, and track deliverables through MasterControl and SharePoint to ensure nothing slips through. I've also built and delivered complete training programs — instructor-led, e-learning, and audio/visual — and assessed post-training competency.

I thrive in regulated environments where precision, version control, and compliance aren't optional. Whether the audience is a maintenance technician on the floor or a QA director reviewing SOPs, I write documentation that works for both.
